Concept Design for Noodl App

Noodl is a node-based prototyping app — have a look at my article about it on Medium if you are wondering more.

In this concept design I visualized some of my feature ideas for Noodl and polished its UI & UX. It’s a working prototype made with Framer. It was really challanging and fun to prototype a prototyping app in a prototyping app! I loved every minutes of it.

I'm planning to write a detailed article on the concept design soon. For now you can see some of the screenshots and a speeded-up video below. (HD version of the video.)
